ABOUT US

Welcome to DDA INTERNATIONAL:
We're a passionate team of Community Service Creators, specializing in Discord bot and Minecraft server development, with a strong background in web, Minecraft, and Discord bot technologies. Our portfolio includes high-quality projects such as the USF bot and the GenesisCore Minecraft SMP, along with many other exciting ventures. We thrive on innovation and the creative thrill that comes from building unique, impactful solutions, rather than pursuing financial gain. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every project we undertake.

Community Services

We have various channels, events, and features to entertain our community. Chat with members who share your interests, suggest new features, play games with friends using our bots, and reach out to support for any questions or issues. Discord Bots

We develop Discord Bots, mostly using JavaScript, with a lot of functions designed for server management and moderation plus some utility and fun features to entertain the community. You can invite our bots to your servers too! We also support the new User-Installed application functions!
